The Strategic Question

Is your biggest pain "I can't coach my reps effectively" (Gong) or "My reps waste hours on CRM updates and follow-up emails" (Sybill)? Both are valid problems. Pick the tool that solves your bigger one.

Gong

Founded 2015 · $584M raised · $7.25B valuation · San Francisco
🏅

The Category Leader

Gong pioneered conversation intelligence and remains the gold standard. If "we use Gong" is table stakes for enterprise sales teams, there's a reason. Their transcription quality, coaching tools, and deal intelligence are best-in-class.

What Gong Does Best

Call Coaching: Gong's coaching tools let managers review calls efficiently, leave timestamped feedback, and track rep improvement over time. Scorecards make coaching systematic.

Deal Intelligence: Deal boards show pipeline health based on actual conversation signals--not just what reps put in Salesforce. See which deals have gone dark, which lack multi-threading.

Transcription: 70+ language support with industry-leading accuracy. If you sell globally, this matters.

Sybill

Founded 2020 · YC-backed · San Francisco
🤖

The Rep Productivity Play

Sybill asks: what if we focused on making reps more productive instead of making managers better coaches? Their AI automatically fills CRM fields, writes follow-up emails, and creates deal summaries--the admin work reps hate.

What Sybill Does Best

CRM Auto-Fill: Sybill doesn't just transcribe--it extracts structured data and populates Salesforce/HubSpot fields automatically. MEDDIC fields, next steps, pain points--captured without rep data entry.

AI Follow-Up Emails: Context-aware follow-up emails written automatically after each call. Not generic templates--emails that reference what was actually discussed.

Deal Summaries: AI-generated deal briefs that combine call context with CRM data. Useful for handoffs and manager syncs.

Decision Framework

Which One Should You Actually Buy?

COACHING CULTURE

"We Need to Build a Coaching Program"

✔

Choose Gong

If call coaching and rep development are strategic priorities, Gong's purpose-built tools are worth the premium. Scorecards, manager dashboards, and systematic feedback loops are what Gong was built for.

REP PRODUCTIVITY

"Our Reps Hate CRM Data Entry"

✔

Choose Sybill

If CRM hygiene is your problem and reps are wasting hours on admin work, Sybill's auto-fill and AI follow-ups directly address that pain. You'll get cleaner pipeline data and happier reps.

BUDGET CONSCIOUS

"We Need CI But Can't Afford Gong"

✔

Choose Sybill

At 3-4x cheaper with monthly contracts and a free trial, Sybill is the accessible option. You won't get Gong's coaching depth, but you'll get solid transcription, CRM automation, and AI follow-ups.

The CRO Report's Bottom Line

Different tools for different priorities:

  • Gong wins at: Call coaching, manager dashboards, deal intelligence, enterprise integrations, and transcription quality. The category leader for a reason.
  • Sybill wins at: CRM automation, AI follow-up emails, rep productivity, affordable pricing, and contract flexibility. The challenger that asks "what if CI was built for reps?"
  • The honest answer: If budget isn't a constraint and coaching is strategic, Gong. If you want CI that makes reps more productive without breaking the bank, Sybill. Try Sybill's free trial before committing to Gong's 2-year contract.

🔍 Questions to Ask Before You Buy

  1. Who is the primary user? Managers needing coaching tools (Gong) or reps needing productivity gains (Sybill)?
  2. What's your realistic budget? Gong's all-in cost ($200K+ for 100 users) vs. Sybill (~$60-120K) is significant.
  3. Can you commit to 2 years? Gong's typical contract. Sybill offers monthly.
  4. How bad is your CRM hygiene? If reps aren't updating Salesforce, Sybill's auto-fill is transformative.
  5. Do you need global language support? Gong's 70+ languages matter for international teams.
