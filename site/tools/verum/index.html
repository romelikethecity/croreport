Company Overview

What Is Verum?

Verum is an AI-powered managed data services company that handles data cleaning, enrichment, deduplication, and analysis for B2B revenue teams. Instead of licensing software you need to configure and maintain, Verum operates as a service‚Äîyou send them data problems, they send back clean data.

The model is fundamentally different from tools like Openprise or LeanData. Those platforms require implementation, ongoing administration, and internal expertise. Verum handles the work for you, combining AI automation with human quality assurance to deliver results without the operational overhead.

Verum competes less with pure software tools and more with the alternative of hiring in-house data ops resources or working with traditional data services agencies. Their pitch: get the results faster and cheaper than doing it yourself.
