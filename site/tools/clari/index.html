Company Overview

What Is Clari?

Clari is a revenue operations platform focused on forecasting accuracy, pipeline visibility, and deal inspection. Founded in 2012 by Andy Byrne and Venkat Rangan, the company has raised $496M and was valued at $2.6B in its 2022 Series F round. They're headquartered in Sunnyvale, California.

The core product connects to your CRM, email, and calendar to give you visibility into every deal's real status. Not what reps say they are‚Äîwhat the data says they are. Are deals going to slip? Is the forecast accurate? Clari tells you before your reps do.

For revenue leaders tired of being surprised by forecast misses, Clari provides the inspection layer that CRMs alone can't. You get forecast accuracy, pipeline governance, deal-level risk signals, and historical trend analysis.

üí°

Built for CROs, Not Individual Reps

Clari's primary users are CROs, VPs of Sales, and RevOps teams‚Äînot individual reps. The dashboards, forecasting models, and pipeline views are designed for leadership visibility. Reps interact with Clari primarily through deal updates and forecast submissions.

Don't Forget Implementation Costs

Unlike some SaaS tools you can "just turn on," Clari requires real implementation work. Plan for $15,000-75,000 in professional services and 8-16 weeks to get fully operational. The platform's value depends on proper configuration and data hygiene.

Negotiation Tips

Based on procurement data from Vendr and user reports:

  • Volume matters: Meaningful discounts begin at 75+ users, with best rates at 150+ and 300+.
  • Multi-year commits: 2-3 year deals can unlock 20-30% discounts.
  • Bundle CI carefully: Clari Copilot is newer and less proven than Gong‚Äînegotiate hard if you're adding it.
  • Implementation scope: Get a fixed-price implementation quote in writing. Scope creep is common.

Limitations

Where Clari Falls Short

Clari is a fantastic forecasting tool. But they've been trying to expand into conversation intelligence with their Copilot product (acquired Wingman in 2022). This add-on isn't as mature as purpose-built tools like Gong.

Clari Copilot: The Catch-Up Play

Clari acquired Wingman in 2022 to build conversation intelligence capabilities. While Copilot is improving, it's still playing catch-up with Gong's more mature offering. If call coaching is your primary need, Gong is the better choice.

Clari is the best revenue forecasting tool on the market. If your problem is forecast accuracy, pipeline visibility, and knowing which deals will actually close‚ÄîClari is worth the investment. The forecasting models, pipeline inspection, and executive dashboards are genuinely excellent.

But know what you're buying:

  • Buy Clari for forecasting and pipeline visibility. It's best-in-class.
  • Don't buy Clari Copilot expecting Gong-level call coaching. It's not there yet.
  • Budget for a real implementation‚Äî8-16 weeks and $15K-75K in services.
  • Clean your CRM data before or during implementation.
  • Ensure you have RevOps capacity to own and maintain the platform.
