Company Overview

What Is AiSDR?

AiSDR is an AI-powered sales development platform that automates outbound prospecting, email sequences, and lead response handling. The platform combines a 700M+ contact database with AI-generated personalized outreach, intent signal monitoring, and automated reply management.

What sets AiSDR apart in the crowded AI SDR market isn't necessarily the technology‚Äîit's the business model. While competitors like 11x require $60K+ annual commitments and Artisan demands year-long contracts, AiSDR offers quarterly billing with cancel-anytime flexibility. This approach acknowledges an uncomfortable truth: AI SDR technology is still maturing, and most buyers don't know if it will work for their specific use case.

The platform handles both outbound prospecting (finding and emailing new leads) and inbound response management (replying to interested prospects). When a lead responds, AiSDR's AI can engage in back-and-forth conversation and book meetings‚Äîwith response times under 10 minutes.

Why Contract Terms Matter for AI SDRs

AI SDR technology is still early. Results vary wildly by industry, ICP, and use case. Committing $60K annually to unproven technology is a significant risk. AiSDR's quarterly contracts let you validate product-market fit before scaling‚Äîthe smart approach in an emerging category.

The Risk Comparison

To test AiSDR for 90 days: $2,700. To test 11x for the minimum commitment: $60,000. To test Artisan: $18,000-24,000 (annual contract required). AiSDR lets you validate AI SDR technology at 1/20th the risk of 11x.

Understanding the Per-Message Cost

At $0.75 per message, costs can add up at scale. Here's what different volumes look like:

  • 1,000 messages/month: $750 in message fees + $900 base = $1,650/month
  • 2,500 messages/month: $1,875 in message fees + $900 base = $2,775/month
  • 5,000 messages/month: $3,750 in message fees + $900 base = $4,650/month

For high-volume outbound teams, the per-message model may become expensive. But for teams testing AI SDR technology or running moderate volumes, it's a reasonable trade-off for flexibility.

Limitations

Where AiSDR Falls Short

AiSDR has the best contract terms in the AI SDR market, but it's not without limitations. Here are the honest trade-offs:

HubSpot-Only CRM Integration

The biggest limitation is CRM support. Currently, AiSDR only integrates with HubSpot. If you're a Salesforce shop, you'll need to wait‚ÄîSalesforce integration is on the roadmap but not yet available. For enterprise teams standardized on Salesforce, this is a dealbreaker.

"We were ready to sign with AiSDR until we learned it's HubSpot only. Our entire sales stack runs on Salesforce, and we can't justify switching CRMs just for one tool. We're waiting for their Salesforce integration."
‚Äî Revenue Operations Manager, Mid-Market Company

Limited Signal Customization

While AiSDR monitors intent signals, the customization options for which signals trigger outreach are more limited than some competitors. Power users may find the signal configuration less flexible than they'd like.

Per-Message Costs at Scale

The $0.75/message fee is reasonable for moderate volumes but becomes expensive at scale. Teams sending 10,000+ messages monthly may find the economics less favorable than competitors with unlimited messaging tiers.

Questions to Ask Before Signing

  1. When is Salesforce integration coming? If you need it, get a timeline in writing before committing.
  2. What's included in the base $900/month? Understand exactly how many messages and features are covered.
  3. How does message rollover work? Confirm unused credits carry forward and for how long.
  4. What's the cancel process? Even with quarterly contracts, understand the specific cancellation procedure.
  5. Can you share case studies from similar companies? Ask for references in your industry and company size.
  6. What happens if I scale past 5,000 messages? Understand volume pricing and whether bulk discounts are available.
