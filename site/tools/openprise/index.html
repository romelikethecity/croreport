Company Overview

What Is Openprise?

Openprise is an enterprise data orchestration platform that handles data cleansing, enrichment, segmentation, and routing across your entire GTM tech stack. Founded in 2014, they've positioned themselves as the "no-code" solution for complex data operations.

The platform connects to virtually every system in your stack‚ÄîCRMs, marketing automation, data warehouses, enrichment vendors‚Äîand orchestrates data flows between them. Think of it as a central nervous system for your revenue data.

Openprise competes with tools like Tray.io, Workato, and custom-built data pipelines. Their differentiator is being purpose-built for RevOps use cases rather than general-purpose automation.

This Is Enterprise Software

Openprise requires a dedicated admin and weeks of implementation. If you're a startup or mid-market company without a RevOps team, this tool will overwhelm you. Consider simpler alternatives first.

The Bottom Line

Openprise is genuinely impressive technology for enterprises that need industrial-strength data orchestration. If you're moving millions of records across dozens of systems and have the team to manage it, it delivers real ROI.

But for most companies, it's overkill. The $35K+ price tag, months-long implementation, and ongoing admin requirements make it impractical unless you're operating at true enterprise scale. Before committing, honestly assess whether your data problems justify this level of investment‚Äîor if simpler, more focused tools would serve you better.
