Company Overview

What Is Regie.ai?

Regie.ai is an AI-powered sales engagement platform that aims to replace the traditional stack of separate prospecting, sequencing, and dialing tools. Founded in 2020 and based in San Francisco, the company has raised over $20M in funding.

The core pitch: bring enrichment, AI content generation, email sequencing, and parallel dialing into one platform. Their "AI Agents" warm up leads before handing them to human reps for calls‚Äîa hybrid human+AI approach.

Regie positions against traditional SEPs like Outreach and Salesloft by emphasizing AI throughout the workflow, not just as a bolt-on feature. The parallel dialer (built into the platform) is a genuine differentiator for teams focused on phone outreach.

The Bottom Line

Regie.ai is a legitimate enterprise platform with a genuine differentiator: the built-in parallel dialer. For phone-heavy outbound teams with $35K+ budgets, the tool consolidation and AI assistance can provide real value.

But the pricing opacity is frustrating, the AI copy quality gets mixed reviews, and there's no way to test before committing. If you're not doing high-volume phone outreach, the parallel dialer premium ($150/rep/month) doesn't make sense‚Äîand there are more flexible alternatives like AiSDR for teams wanting to test AI SDR without the enterprise commitment.
