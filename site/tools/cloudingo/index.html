Company Overview

What Is Cloudingo?

Cloudingo is a data cleaning tool built specifically for Salesforce. It focuses primarily on deduplication‚Äîfinding and merging duplicate leads, contacts, accounts, and custom objects within your Salesforce org.

The company has been around since 2011, making it one of the more established players in the Salesforce data quality space. They've built their reputation on being easy to use and having strong safety features (like the ability to undo merges).

Cloudingo competes with Salesforce's native duplicate management, DemandTools, Duplicate Check, and broader data quality platforms like Openprise. Their niche is being affordable and focused‚Äîthey don't try to be everything to everyone.

The Undo Feature Is Underrated

Cloudingo's ability to restore merged records is genuinely valuable. If you've ever accidentally merged the wrong records and had to manually recreate data, you'll appreciate this safety net.

The Bottom Line

Cloudingo is the reliable specialist for Salesforce deduplication. It doesn't try to be a full data operations platform‚Äîit does one thing well and charges fairly for it. The undo feature alone makes it worth considering over native Salesforce deduplication.
