Company Overview

What Is Demandbase?

Demandbase is an enterprise account-based marketing (ABM) platform that combines account intelligence, intent data, advertising, and sales intelligence into a unified solution. Founded in 2006, they've grown through acquisition (Engagio, InsideView, TechTarget Priority Engine) to become one of the most comprehensive‚Äîand expensive‚ÄîABM platforms on the market.

The platform's core value proposition is helping enterprise B2B companies identify, target, and engage their best-fit accounts. They provide proprietary intent data that shows which companies are actively researching topics relevant to your products.

Demandbase competes with 6sense, Bombora, and ZoomInfo on intent data, while competing with Terminus on ABM advertising. Their attempt to be "all-in-one" means they're broad but not always deepest in each category.

This Is Enterprise Software with Enterprise Pricing

Demandbase starts around $30K/year for basic packages but quickly scales to $100K-$200K+ for full platform access. If you're not prepared for a six-figure annual commitment and months of implementation, look elsewhere.

The Bottom Line

Demandbase is the Mercedes-Benz of ABM platforms: premium quality, premium price, and you need to know how to drive it to get value. The intent data is genuinely differentiated, and the platform is comprehensive for enterprise ABM programs with resources to operate it.

But for most companies, Demandbase is overkill. The six-figure price tags, multi-year contracts, and implementation complexity put it out of reach‚Äîor make it a poor investment‚Äîfor anyone without a mature, well-funded ABM operation. Consider simpler alternatives (like point solutions for intent data or advertising) before committing to the full platform.
