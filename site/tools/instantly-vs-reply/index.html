The Strategic Question

Is email your primary (or only) outbound channel? Instantly. Do you need LinkedIn and calls integrated into the same sequences? Reply.io. Most teams start email-only and add channels later--so Instantly often makes sense to start.

Instantly

Founded 2021 · Cold email platform · Bootstrapped
📧

The Cold Email Specialist

Instantly's bet: if you do cold email, do it at scale with maximum deliverability. Unlimited email accounts, built-in warmup, and inbox rotation let you send volume without destroying sender reputation.

What Sets Instantly Apart

Unlimited Sending Accounts: Connect as many email accounts as you want. Rotate between them automatically. This is how agencies send tens of thousands of emails without landing in spam.

Built-in Warmup: New domains need warming before you can send volume. Instantly's warmup network is included--no extra cost, no separate tool needed.

Deliverability Focus: Smart sending, inbox rotation, and warmup combined mean better deliverability than platforms that treat email as just one channel.

Reply.io

Founded 2014 · Multichannel sales engagement · Ukraine
💬

The Multichannel Platform

Reply.io's bet: modern outbound requires multiple channels working together. Email, LinkedIn, calls, and WhatsApp in coordinated sequences. One platform, one workflow.

What Sets Reply.io Apart

Multichannel Sequences: Build sequences that combine email, LinkedIn connection requests, LinkedIn messages, calls, and even WhatsApp. Prospects get touched across channels automatically.

LinkedIn Automation: Native LinkedIn integration for connection requests, messages, and profile views. No separate tool needed.

Jason AI: AI assistant that helps with email writing, research, and even handling replies. More than just templates.

✔

AGENCY / HIGH VOLUME

✔

Agencies running cold email at scale need unlimited sending accounts and inbox rotation. Instantly was built for this exact use case. Reply.io's per-user pricing gets expensive fast.

The CRO Report's Bottom Line

Different tools for different outbound motions:

  • Instantly wins at: Cold email at scale, unlimited sending accounts, built-in warmup, deliverability, and affordable pricing. The go-to for agencies and email-first teams.
  • Reply.io wins at: Multichannel sequences, LinkedIn automation, built-in calling, CRM integrations, and consolidated workflows. The go-to for in-house teams wanting one platform.
  • The honest answer: If email is 80%+ of your outbound, start with Instantly. If you need LinkedIn and calls integrated, Reply.io saves you from stitching together multiple tools.

🔍 Questions to Ask Before You Buy

  1. What percentage of your outbound is email-only? If 80%+, Instantly. If multichannel matters, Reply.io.
  2. How many emails do you send monthly? High volume favors Instantly's unlimited accounts.
  3. Do you need LinkedIn automation? Only Reply.io has this native.
  4. What's your budget per user? Instantly is significantly cheaper for email-only use.
  5. Do you need Salesforce/HubSpot integration? Reply.io has deeper CRM connections.
