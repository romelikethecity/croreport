About The CRO Report

The executive sales job market is opaque, as are the tools. Salary data hides behind "competitive compensation." Company intel is scattered across press releases, Glassdoor, LinkedIn, and investor updates. Nobody analyzes it, and nobody tells you what actually matters.

Tool Reviews

Every GTM tool comparison follows the same process:

  1. Primary research: We use the tools ourselves where possible
  2. User feedback: G2, Capterra, Reddit, and direct outreach to current users
  3. Pricing verification: Published pricing, user-reported costs, and negotiation platforms like Vendr
  4. News and investigations: TechCrunch, Forbes, SEC filings for public companies
  5. Red flag identification: Contract terms, churn signals, support quality

We may receive affiliate compensation from tools we review. Our analysis is independent. If a tool isn't worth your budget, we'll tell you. Every source is linked.

Our Approach

What we believe:

  • Data beats opinions
  • Honest analysis includes red flags
  • Revenue leaders deserve investor-grade intelligence
  • Compensation transparency benefits everyone

What we don't do:

  • Soft-pedal concerns because a company is "prestigious"
  • Recycle press releases as analysis
  • Pretend we know things we don't

If we see restructurings, poor user reviews, below-market comp, leadership turnover, or Glassdoor warnings - we tell you.

About Rome Thorndike

Rome Thorndike is the founder of The CRO Report and VP of Revenue at Firmograph.ai, where he builds AI agents that analyze GTM data for CROs and RevOps leaders.

His career spans 15+ years in B2B sales and revenue leadership: enterprise sales at Salesforce and Microsoft, helping scale Sequoia-backed Snapdocs from Series A through Series D, and leading sales at Datajoy through its acquisition by Databricks.

Rome holds an MBA from UC Berkeley's Haas School of Business, where he studied statistical analysis and machine learning. He's based in the San Francisco Bay Area and also publishes The AI News Digest, a top-ranked technology newsletter.
