Understanding MEDDPICC

01

What is MEDDPICC?

A qualification framework to assess deal health and identify knowledge gaps
Identifies blind spots in your opportunities before they derail your deal
Creates a common language across your sales organization and leadership
Maintains forecast accuracy through systematic, data-driven qualification
Not a checklist — it's a journey throughout the entire sales process

The Three Whys

02

Every Deal Must Answer Three Questions

Why Do Anything?

What's driving them to change from the status quo? Without compelling pain, there's no motivation to act.

Why Us?

What makes your solution the right choice over competitors and alternatives? What's your differentiator?

Why Now?

What creates urgency to act today rather than next quarter? What's the cost of delay?

THE CRO REPORT

Champion vs. Coach

Champion

  • Has power and influence
  • Has a personal stake in your win
  • Actively sells when you're not there
  • Can articulate the 3 Whys
  • Returns your calls proactively

Coach

  • Provides information
  • May not have decision power
  • Helps you navigate, not sell
  • May be friendly but passive
  • Limited influence with EB
THE CRO REPORT

MEDDPICC

THE CRO REPORT

Competitive Strategies

DIRECT — When you have overwhelming advantage (3:1). Default strategy.
DIVIDE — Fragment the competition. "Beach Head" or "Trojan Horse" approach.
INDIRECT — When competition is too strong. Change the ground with new buying influences.
DELAY — When losing. Slow the deal to change MEDDPICC dynamics.

Mastering Champions

04

The Champion Definition

Power & Influence

They command the room and have access to decision makers

Personal Win

They have something to gain from your success

Sells for You

They advocate for you when you're not in the room

You need evidence of all three to have a true champion.

THE CRO REPORT

How to Spot a Champion

They answer the tough questions with authority
They're not afraid to say "I don't know"
They've led transformational projects before
They're hard to reach (high demand)
New to the organization with something to prove
THE CRO REPORT

Testing Your Champion

Can they articulate the 3 Whys without your guidance?
Can they give you access to the EB and run meetings for you?
Do they provide competitive intelligence proactively?
Do they return your calls? Do they call YOU?
Play the negative: "I'm not sure we can get this done..." — see how they respond
